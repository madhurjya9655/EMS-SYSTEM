Leave Policy
  • Annual Quota: 24 paid leaves per year (Apr 1 – Mar 31). Days beyond 24 are counted as unpaid leave.
  • Deduction on Apply: Leave balance is deducted as soon as you apply (pending status). Rejected leaves restore your balance.
  • Half-day: Half-day leaves do NOT deduct from your 24-leave quota.
  • Types: Casual Leave, Maternity Leave, Compensatory Off.
  • Approval: Your reporting person must approve or reject. Email token links are also supported.
  • Overlap: Overlapping requests are blocked automatically.
  • Tasks: Approved & pending leaves auto-skip your planned tasks. No tasks on holidays or Sundays.
  • Audit: Every action is logged with timestamp, IP and actor.
