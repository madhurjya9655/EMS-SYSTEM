Date & time
  • Preferred date format: YYYY-MM-DD (ISO).

Holiday rules
  • Dates in Settings → Holiday List (including Saturday-offs) are treated as non-working.
  • First occurrences (from forms or bulk upload) can fall on any day, including Sundays / holidays.
  • For recurring checklists, if the next occurrence falls on a Sunday/holiday, it is moved to the next working day at 7:00 PM IST.
  • Delegations are one-time; they are not automatically re-scheduled or rolled.
