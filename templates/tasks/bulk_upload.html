Holiday rules
  • Dates added in Settings → Holiday List (incl. monthly Saturday-offs) are treated as non-working.
  • Checklist: If the selected/uploaded date is a holiday/Sunday, it moves to the next working day and keeps your chosen time-of-day (IST).
  • Delegation: If the selected/uploaded datetime is a holiday/Sunday, it moves to the next working day and keeps your chosen time-of-day (IST).
  • If the shifted date is also a holiday, it continues forward until a working day is found.
